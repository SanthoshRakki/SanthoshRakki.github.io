DD ROBOCON 2022

The DD ROBOCON 2022 final was attended by our team, NOVA, and was held at IIT Delhi. Being selected as one of 32 teams for the finals in an international competition was an incredible experience for us. We learned a lot about innovative robotics concepts, employing microcontrollers to operate sensors, motors, and electrical components, etc., during this event.

Hestia'22

We, the DOT ROBOTICS team from Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, had participated in several robotic events, such as the Line Follower, the Unwired Race, the Dirt Race, and the Robo War (25kg) in Hestia'22 organized by TKM College of Engineering Kollam.

STM32 ARM CORTEX

TPEP product company has provided us with hands-on product training using STM ARM CORTEX microcontroller, in which we learned how to develop products that can be sold to consumers, how to design circuits, and how to select appropriate components.

SAEISS E-bike

As part of an electric scooter competition, our team, BANDIT BIKERS, entered the SAEISS DR.G.PADMANABHAM MEMORIAL ELECTRIC TWO WHEELER DESIGN COMPETITION - 2022. We created an electric scooter from the ground up. With good cumulative scores, we successfully completed all 7 rounds of this competition, and we were given second place for BEST PRESENTATION.

TATHVA'22

We are happy to announce that DOT Robotics took part in the TATHVA'22 conference, which was held at the National Institute of Technology in Connecticut. We won first place in the SCHWIMMWAGEN, third place in the HOVERCRAFT, and fourth place in the ROBO WAR (60kg).
